Our Fleet Repair Process

Efficient, transparent, and thorough fleet vehicle repair

1

Initial Assessment

We perform a comprehensive inspection of your fleet vehicle to identify all issues requiring attention. Our technicians use advanced diagnostic equipment to ensure nothing is missed.

2

Detailed Estimate

You'll receive a detailed estimate outlining all recommended repairs, associated costs, and estimated completion time. We prioritize transparency so there are never any surprises.

3

Approval & Scheduling

Once you approve the estimate, we'll schedule the repairs based on your business needs and our current workload, with priority scheduling for fleet customers.

4

Quality Repairs

Our certified technicians perform all necessary repairs using quality parts and materials. We follow manufacturer specifications and industry best practices for all work.

5

Quality Control

Every repaired vehicle undergoes a thorough quality control inspection to ensure all work meets our high standards before being returned to service.

6

Documentation & Delivery

We provide detailed documentation of all repairs performed, along with recommendations for future maintenance. Your vehicle is then cleaned and returned to service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our fleet repair services

What types of fleet vehicles do you service?

We service all types of commercial fleet vehicles, including delivery vans, box trucks, pickup trucks, service vehicles, company cars, and specialty vehicles like food trucks or refrigerated vehicles. Our facility can accommodate vehicles of various sizes, and our technicians are trained to work on all makes and models.

Do you offer mobile repair services for fleet vehicles?

Yes, we offer mobile repair services for fleet customers. Our mobile repair units can perform many maintenance and repair services at your location, minimizing downtime for your business. This service is available for both emergency repairs and scheduled maintenance within the Philadelphia area.

How do your fleet maintenance plans work?

Our fleet maintenance plans are subscription-based services that provide regular maintenance and inspections for your vehicles at a predictable monthly cost. Each plan includes different levels of service, from basic quarterly inspections to comprehensive monthly maintenance. All plans include discounts on additional repairs and priority scheduling. We can customize a plan based on your fleet size, vehicle types, and specific business needs.

What are the benefits of your fleet account management?

Our fleet account management services streamline the administrative aspects of fleet maintenance. Benefits include consolidated billing for all your vehicles, detailed documentation of all services performed, maintenance history tracking, cost analysis reports, and a dedicated account manager for larger fleets. This system helps you track expenses, plan for future maintenance, and simplify the accounting process for your vehicle-related costs.

How quickly can you service fleet vehicles?

Fleet customers receive priority scheduling at our facility. Turnaround times depend on the type and extent of repairs needed, but we strive to complete most maintenance services within the same day. For more extensive repairs, we provide a detailed timeline estimate and keep you updated on progress. We understand that vehicle downtime impacts your business, so we work efficiently without compromising quality.

Do you offer fleet branding and graphics services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive fleet branding services, including custom paint jobs, vinyl wraps, decals, and logo application. We can work with your existing brand guidelines or help develop a consistent look for your fleet. Our painting facilities can accommodate vehicles of all sizes, and we use high-quality materials designed to withstand commercial use and maintain your company's professional image.
